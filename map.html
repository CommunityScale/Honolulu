Housing Plan Map

This map was created by CommunityScale for the County & City of Honolulu. The map integrates key observations and findings for City staff, stakeholders, and the public to use. Mapped layers focus on the most important and impactful data to help viewers understand and appreciate the critical relationships between housing development potential and factors such as zoning, transit access, and proximity to services.

Honolulu Mayor’s Office of Housing drives policy and strategy for housing islandwide. The office leads cross-sector collaboration and policy change to stimulate the acquisition, development, and preservation of housing for all of Oahu’s residents, including the creation of pathways from homelessness to housing.
